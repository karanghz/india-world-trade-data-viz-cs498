India's World Trade

An overview of India's world trade in the last 10 years, 2009 to 2018.

By Karan Gupta | About This Visualisation
Let us start by looking at India's Balance of Trade with respect to each country

This world map shows India's 10 year trade balance for each country in the world. The countries coloured in shades of red are the ones from which India has imported a lot more than it has exported. On the other hand, there are many countries to which India mostly exports goods.
Hover over a country to view it's 10 year import and export volume in million USD.





Now let's see how India's Annual Export/Import volume has varied in the last 10 years

It is interesting to note that the export and import amounts are highly correlated. This can potentially act as a proxy for the state of the global economy.
