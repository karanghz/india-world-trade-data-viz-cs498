About "India's World Trade"

Did India maintain a Balance of Trade with the different countries of the World?

Not with all! This world map shows India's 10 year trade balance for each country of the world.
The countries coloured in dark red are the ones from which India has imported a lot more than it has exported.





Has India's Annual Export/Import varied in the last 10 years?

Yes! In fact, it is interesting to note that the export and import amounts are highly correlated.
